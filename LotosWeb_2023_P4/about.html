Founders

Meet Cassey and Jamie, the visionary duo behind Lotos Fragrance, a nature-inspired perfume company that has captured the essence of their shared passion for the environment. United by a love for the outdoors and a commitment to sustainability, they founded Lotos to redefine the fragrance industry. Frustrated by the prevalence of synthetic and harmful ingredients in traditional perfumes, they sought to create a line of scents that not only celebrated the beauty of nature but also prioritized eco-friendly and cruelty-free practices. With Lotos Fragrance, Cassey and Jamie aim to inspire a connection to the natural world through the transformative power of fragrance.
